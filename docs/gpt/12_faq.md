# Frequently Asked Questions

## What industries can use DeepMine forecasting?

- Manufacturing
- Retail
- Finance
- Logistics
- Energy

---

## What data is required?

Minimum:

- Historical target variable

Recommended:

- Sales
- Inventory
- Weather
- Promotions
- Economic indicators

---

## Can exchange rates be predicted?

Partially.

Short-term forecasts may be possible.

Long-term forecasts are highly uncertain.

---

## Why use foundation models?

Benefits:

- Faster deployment
- Better baseline performance
- Reduced training cost

---

## What is the difference between ARIMA and TimeGPT?

ARIMA:

- Statistical
- Interpretable

TimeGPT:

- Foundation model
- Large-scale pretraining
- Better generalization
