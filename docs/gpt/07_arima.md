# ARIMA

## Definition

ARIMA stands for:

- AutoRegression
- Integrated
- Moving Average

Notation:

ARIMA(p,d,q)

## Components

### AR

Uses past values.

### I

Differencing.

### MA

Uses past forecast errors.

## Requirements

ARIMA requires stationary data.

## Advantages

- Interpretable
- Reliable
- Strong baseline

## Limitations

- Linear assumptions
- Limited handling of complex patterns
