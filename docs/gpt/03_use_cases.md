# Use Cases

## Manufacturing

### Product Demand Forecasting

Predict future product demand to optimize production planning.

Benefits:

- Reduced inventory
- Reduced stockouts
- Improved procurement

---

## Service Parts Forecasting

Forecast spare parts demand.

Benefits:

- Lower inventory cost
- Improved service quality

---

## Exchange Rate Forecasting

Predict future USD/KRW exchange rates.

Benefits:

- Better purchasing timing
- Reduced foreign exchange risk

---

## Commodity Forecasting

Predict raw material prices.

Examples:

- Copper
- Aluminum
- Steel
- Oil

---

## Retail Forecasting

Predict future sales volume.

Benefits:

- Better stock allocation
- Promotion planning

---

## Financial Forecasting

Predict:

- Product demand
- Price movements
- Market indicators
