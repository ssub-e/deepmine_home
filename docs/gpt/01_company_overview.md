# DeepMine Company Overview

## Company Summary

DeepMine is an AI company specializing in demand forecasting, time-series analysis, predictive analytics, and large-scale AI model development.

Founded in 2021, the company provides forecasting solutions for manufacturing, retail, finance, logistics, exchange rates, commodity prices, and service parts management.

## Core Capabilities

### Demand Forecasting

- Product demand forecasting
- Service parts demand forecasting
- Installed base forecasting
- Inventory forecasting

### Financial Forecasting

- Exchange rate forecasting
- Commodity price forecasting
- Financial product demand forecasting

### Manufacturing Analytics

- Yield prediction
- Process optimization
- Production planning

## Key Technologies

- Machine Learning
- Deep Learning
- Foundation Forecasting Models
- Large Language Models
- Time Series Analytics
