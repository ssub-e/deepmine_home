# Deep Learning Forecasting

## Why Deep Learning

Traditional models analyze individual time series.

Deep learning learns patterns across many series simultaneously.

## Major Architectures

### MLP

Examples:

- NBEATS
- NHITS

### RNN

Examples:

- LSTM
- GRU
- DeepAR

### CNN

Examples:

- BiTCN

### Transformer

Examples:

- TFT
- Autoformer
- PatchTST

## Benefits

- Higher scalability
- Complex pattern recognition
- Better use of exogenous variables
