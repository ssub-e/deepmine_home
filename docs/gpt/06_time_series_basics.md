# Time Series Fundamentals

## Definition

Time series data consists of observations recorded over time.

Examples:

- Daily sales
- Monthly demand
- Exchange rates
- Electricity consumption

## Components

### Trend

Long-term movement.

### Seasonality

Repeated patterns.

### Cyclic Patterns

Economic cycles.

### Noise

Random fluctuations.

## Forecast Horizon

### Short-term

Days to weeks.

### Medium-term

Months.

### Long-term

Years.

## Forecast Accuracy Drivers

- Data quality
- Historical consistency
- External factors
- Predictability of the domain
